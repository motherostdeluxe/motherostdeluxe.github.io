MOTHER / ORIGINAL SOUNDTRACK

This new edition is divided into four "discs":

THE REMASTERED ALBUM /

The album you know and love, fully remastered from the original 1989 CD, with unprecedented sound quality and totally redefined stereo imaging.

THE RARITIES /

A collection of demos and excerpts from the album's recording sessions, most of which are hard to find! Restored with care for the best listening experience.

THE INSTRUMENTAL ALBUM /

All songs with vocals are now vocals-free! Enjoy the outstanding arrangements created for this album ;) (AI-powered)

THE UNUSUAL MIXES /

Strange mixes of the entire album, to enjoy this piece of art in a different way. (AI-powered)

N.B.: The AI-powered mixes were produced with the HELP of atificial intelligence models, but the results were retouched by hand for the cleanest possible result. Some audio artifacts inherent to this method of musical separation may nevertheless remain

For obvious copyright reasons, the album is NOT available for download. Instead, you can listen to each song from this website, or directly from the YouTube playlist.
